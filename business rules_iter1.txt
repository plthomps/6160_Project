Business Rules: 

1. One owner can have many pets, but pets can have one and only one owner.
2. One customer can take many services 
3. Each order will have one and only one service associated with it.
4. Consideration for customers who are teachers and students.
5. One service can have many technicians, but one technician can work with one service at a time.
6. One technician can have many certifications.
7. One technician can have many ratings, but each rating is uniquely associated with one technician.
8. Customer can be a technician and vice versa.
